Lesson 3 - Job security in Creative Digital Media

3 tips on how to get a job and how to keep a job in Creative Digital Media


Tip 1 - Tech-junkies succeed

With more companies looking to create apps than ever, jobs devoted solely to Creative Digital Media are popping up all over the place. A small bit of knowledge of computer systems and coding will go a long way in an interview.

Tip 2 - Don't be afraid to show off your Portfolio

You have the knowledge and the ability. But, for whatever reason, you may not always want to show it off. If you want that job, you cannot let that happen. If you want a job in Creative Digital Media, then be brash and be creative. Your Portfolio can never be a half-measure.

Tip 3 - Always Upskill

Upskilling is a vital part of any job, but in the growing market of Creative Digital Media there will always be competition. People can learn these skills at an astonishing rate and if you want to keep on top, then use the headstart you have now and keep climbing.
