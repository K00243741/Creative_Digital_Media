Lesson 1 - Preparation in Creative Digital Media

3 easy steps to keep in mind before doing a Creative Digital Media project


Tip 1 - Mind Map

Creating mind maps are great ways of ensuring you have enough material for a Creative Digital Media project. Let your mind flow!

Tip 2 - Talk with others

When you're about to start a Creative Digital Media project keep in mind that no one is an island. Other people, from all sorts of backgrounds, may just be able to think up or point out something you would never have thought of on your own.

Tip 3 - Online research

The internet gives you the world at your fingertips. Not every design has to be your own new unique idea. Inspiration from online projects should always be considered.
