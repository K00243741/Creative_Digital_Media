Lesson 2 - Design process efficiency in Creative Digital Media

3 tips on how to efficiently create a Creative Digital Media project


Tip 1 - Go on breaks!

The most obvious tip, but often overlooked, breaks are a vital part of working. They help refresh and organise your brain. And when yopu go back you may just see your work in a new light.

Tip 2 - Keep to the specifications (if you have them)

If you have been given specification for your Creative Digital Media project, then STICK TO THEM! The need to be creative is strong but put that off your past-times. When it comes to jobs, the customer is always right.

Tip 3 - Avoid the bog at all times!

By avoid the bog, I mean do not get bogged down in your work. Being bogged down is miserable. You can't think, or you overthink. You can't do anything but you have everything to do. Get some air, stick to what you've prepared (mind0maps, etc) and keep in mind that perfection is not required.
